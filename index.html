Loans. Recoveries.

Himachal Pradesh Financial Corporation (HPFC) is a statutory corporation formed in 1967 . This corporation was formed under the State Financial Corporations Act, 1951.
This corporation gave refinance loans to entrepreneurs across Himachal Pradesh. However, the loaning business has ceased since 2011. Presently, this Corporation does not give any more loans.
